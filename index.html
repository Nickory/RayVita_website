Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about RayVita

How accurate is the health monitoring without wearable devices?

RayVita uses advanced photoplethysmography (PPG) and computer vision algorithms to achieve clinical-grade accuracy for many metrics. Our technology has been validated against medical-grade equipment with a correlation of over 95% for heart rate monitoring and over 90% for respiratory rate. For more specialized metrics, the app clearly indicates confidence levels and may suggest confirmatory measurements when needed.

Is my health data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All health data processing happens on your device, and any data that is stored in the cloud is end-to-end encrypted. You have complete control over what data is collected and can delete it at any time. RayVita does not sell your data to third parties and complies with all major health data privacy regulations including HIPAA and GDPR.

How often should I use the app for best results?

For optimal results, we recommend daily check-ins that take just 60 seconds. However, even using the app 2-3 times per week provides valuable insights. The more you use RayVita, the more accurate your digital twin becomes, and the more personalized your recommendations will be. The app includes unobtrusive reminders that you can customize to fit your schedule.

Can RayVita replace medical devices or doctor visits?

RayVita is designed as a complementary health tool, not a replacement for professional medical care. While our technology provides valuable insights into your health trends and can detect potential issues early, it should be used alongside regular medical check-ups. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. RayVita can provide useful data to share with your healthcare provider to enhance your care.

Will RayVita work with my smartphone?

RayVita is compatible with most modern smartphones. For iOS, we support iPhone 8 and newer running iOS 14+. For Android, we support devices with Android 9.0+ that have a camera with at least 8MP resolution. Some advanced features may require specific sensors that are not available on all devices. The app will automatically detect your device capabilities and adjust functionality accordingly.
